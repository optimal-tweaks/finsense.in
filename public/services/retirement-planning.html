Retirement Planning

It's never too early to plan for retirement. We help you evaluate all options and identify important elements of all your future requirements. Let’s draft a retirement strategy that’s uncluttered and practical.

How much money do you want before your retirement kicks in? Most of the people would answer as much as possible. But we all know this is not how life works and especially when something is related to money or finances. Retirement planning is an essential part of financial planning. An increase in average life expectancy increases the need for retirement planning. Planning  for retirement not only ensures an additional source of income but also helps in dealing with medical emergencies, fulfil life aspirations and be financially independent. Finsense guides individuals to plan their retirement. It creates a personalized retirement plan and monitors it periodically to ensure the investor is on the right track.

Do you know 2 out of 3 Indians are not prepared to finance their after retirement life. In fact 7 out of 10 Indians expect children to support them in retirement. This all together makes planning for retirement utmost important.

Myths about
retirement planning?

Receiving financial support from children

It may be an unrealistic expectation on the part of many and with the rise in nuclear families this seems far from doom and gloom for most.

Existing savings are sufficient

Inability to anticipate future financial needs is the major roadblock to planning. You need to be proactive and plan for your own retirement.

Would manage later when the time comes

This is one of the biggest mistakes by people who do not plan in advance and let fate decide the future. Most people don’t end up saving for their retirement, assuming that when the time comes, employer benefits like PPF and insurance will be enough. However, they will not cover all your retirement needs.

No plan is necessary for investments

Another biggest investment mistake is saving without an investment plan. Earmark retirement as a goal separate from your other financial goals. Work with a financial advisor who will craft a goal-based plan which will help you live comfortably now and in the future.
