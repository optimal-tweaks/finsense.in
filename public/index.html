Frequently Asked Questions

Are you SEBI Registered?

Yes, Finsense is a SEBI registered investment advisor company. SEBI RIA No. - INH0000033XX

What types of investments do you look at?

We predominantly advise on public market investments which includes Stocks, Mutual Funds, Bonds, ETFs, REITs, FDs and Insurance products.

Who are your current clients?

Current clients include HNIs, NRIs, entrepreneurs, corporate and retail.

How do you charge?

We charge fees as a % on Assets under Advice (AuA) on quarterly basis directly to the client.

How do I know that our incentives are aligned?

We only recommend investments that we know inside out and with the fee based model – your growth is our growth.

Do you also provide implementation services?

Client has the option to avail our implementation services. It includes setting up your mutual fund accounts, managing your investments, supporting you with platforms to transact and view investments, sending you monthly reports, helping you to consolidate insurance policies and integrating the investments for planning reviews.
